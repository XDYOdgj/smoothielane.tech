Luminara Flameless LED Pillar Candles

Bring the warmth and charm of traditional candlelight to your home or outdoor space with these elegant, flameless LED pillar candles. Their realistic flickering flame creates a cozy, inviting ambiance, making every moment feel special without the risks of open flames.

Key Features

  • Realistic flickering flame for a cozy ambiance
  • Battery operated for safe and worry-free use
  • Stone grey color adds a modern touch to any decor
  • Adjustable timer function for energy efficiency
  • Durable design for both indoor and outdoor settings

Why Choose Us?

These candles are not only beautiful but also incredibly practical. With a 100% paraffin wax finish, they are safe for indoor use and free from soot and harmful chemicals, making them a great choice for families with kids and pets. The LED technology allows for long-lasting illumination, with up to 600 hours of runtime on 2 C batteries. Their timer function automatically cycles on for 5 hours and off for 19 hours, helping you save energy and enjoy your space effortlessly. Whether you're gifting them for birthdays, holidays, or special events, they come in sturdy boxes that make wrapping and giving a breeze.
