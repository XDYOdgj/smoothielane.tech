Viking G5 2nd Generation Gate Opener Control Panel

Upgrade your gate automation with the Viking G5 2nd Generation — a reliable, versatile, and modern control panel designed for double swing gate systems. Whether you're looking for simplicity, safety, or energy efficiency, this panel has you covered.

Key Features

  • Designed for double swing gate systems with support for both 24V DC and 24V AC/solar compatibility.
  • Includes a built-in safety photocell to detect obstacles and automatically stop or reverse the gate, ensuring safe and smooth operation for all users.
  • High load capacity, handling gates up to 2000 lbs for 10' gates or 800 lbs for 18' gates — ideal for a wide range of installations.
  • Easy to install using low voltage wiring, reducing setup time and complexity for both new setups and system upgrades.
  • Durable, weather-resistant construction that ensures longevity and reliability even in harsh outdoor conditions.
