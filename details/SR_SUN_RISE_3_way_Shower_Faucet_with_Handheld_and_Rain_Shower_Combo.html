SR SUN RISE 3-way Shower Faucet

Elevate your bathroom experience with this sleek and modern 3-way shower system. Designed for both new installations and bathroom remodeling, it combines style with advanced functionality.

Effortless Functionality for Every Family Member

This 3-way shower faucet offers a rain shower and a handheld shower combo, giving you the flexibility to choose the right setting for any moment. The 6-pattern shower modes make it easy to customize your water flow and comfort level. Whether you're rinsing, relaxing, or saving water, the system adapts to your needs.

Advanced Safety and Performance

With a pressure balance valve, this faucet ensures consistent temperature control, preventing scalding and water hammer noise for a safer, quieter shower. The patented 3-way diverter with anti-swivel lock nut provides a secure and adjustable setup, keeping everything stable during use.

Premium Design Meets Modern Aesthetics

Featuring a durable brushed nickel finish, the SR SUN RISE 3-way faucet blends effortlessly with your bathroom decor. It's the perfect choice for those looking to upgrade their fixtures with both style and substance.

This all-in-one system includes a high-pressure shower valve and cartridge, ensuring long-lasting performance and reliability. The adjustable overhead bracket allows you to position the shower head at your ideal angle, making it convenient for everyone in the family.
