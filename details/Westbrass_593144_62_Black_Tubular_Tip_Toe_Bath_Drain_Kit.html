Westbrass 593144-62 Black Tubular Tip-Toe Drain Kit

This Westbrass black tubular tip-toe drain kit is designed for both convenience and reliability in your bathroom. Featuring 1-1/2 inch x 14 inch black polypropylene tubes, it offers durable performance and a seamless, leak-proof fit for your sink or bathtub. The slip joint washer seal ensures a secure connection and prevents water damage, making it a dependable choice for daily use.

The ADA-approved design is especially beneficial for those with mobility challenges, as it allows for easy, single-handed operation. Included in the kit is a two-hole faceplate with color-matching brass screws that not only enhance the visual appeal of your bathroom but also provide a sturdy and functional installation. The two-hole elbow, shoe, and tee ensure a perfect fit in different bathroom setups, giving you flexibility in installation.

The heavy-duty metal trim adds a touch of modern elegance while ensuring long-term durability and strength. Whether you're upgrading your bathroom or replacing an existing drain plug, this professional-grade kit is built to last, delivering long-term value and satisfaction to your home or business.
