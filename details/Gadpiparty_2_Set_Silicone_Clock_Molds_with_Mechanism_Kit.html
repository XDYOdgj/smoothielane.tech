Create Your Own Custom Wall Clock with Gadpiparty DIY Kit

The Gadpiparty 2 Set Silicone Clock Molds with Mechanism Kit is the ultimate solution for anyone wanting to make or repair a wall clock. This comprehensive set includes silicone mold inserts, a battery-operated quartz movement, two clock hands, two shafts, and two hooks, giving you everything you need to craft a functional and stylish timepiece. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced crafter, this kit empowers you to bring your unique clock designs to life.

What's in the Box?

  • Two sets of high-quality silicone clock molds
  • A ready-to-use battery-operated quartz clock movement
  • Two clock hands for precise time tracking
  • Two clock shafts for secure mounting
  • Two hooks for hanging your wall clock

Perfect for Personalized Wall Clock Decoration

With the versatility of this DIY clock making kit, you can experiment with different materials and styles to create a one-of-a-kind wall clock. Pour your chosen resin, add beads, sequins, or dried flowers for a beautiful and decorative effect. Once the resin cures, simply twist and release your wall clock from the mold. This product is great for creative home decor, office space styling, or as a thoughtful gift for someone who loves crafting.

Why Choose This Kit?

Crafting a wall clock has never been easier. This kit is designed with both functionality and creativity in mind, offering a modular setup that allows you to build clocks in various sizes and formats. The easy demolding process ensures a hassle-free experience, while the included quartz movement guarantees accurate timekeeping. It's an ideal choice for anyone interested in clock mechanism replacement or resin clock mold kit projects.
